Our Journey

Project StoryLines

Our internship journey has been a collaborative exploration into the realm of social media analysis, where our team worked together to harness various tools and techniques for data collection, cleansing, and visualization. We started with web scraping, utilizing BeautifulSoup and Selenium to extract data from websites efficiently. These foundational skills were crucial for the tasks ahead.

As a team, we delved into Power Query in Excel, connecting to datasets and applying comprehensive data cleansing steps. We removed unnecessary rows and columns, replaced missing values, and extracted specific information, preparing the data for deeper analysis. This collaborative effort ensured that our data was streamlined and ready for the next stages.

We also explored Jira software for project management. Together, we created a product backlog, managed tasks, and coordinated sprints. This organization helped us keep track of our workflow and progress, ensuring we stayed on schedule and aligned with our goals. Cleaning the social media dataset using Power Query was a critical step, involving the removal of null values, the addition of relevant columns, and thorough preparation for analysis.

Our work with pandas in Python was a significant team effort. We concatenated datasets, handled various data formats, and performed advanced analyses, including converting different units to a common format and conducting category-wise and country-wise analyses. The decision to switch from a Twitter dataset to a Music Artist dataset was made collectively to ensure the data's relevance and usefulness.

In addition to data cleansing and analysis, we collectively gained proficiency in Power BI. We created interactive dashboards with maps, line charts, and bar graphs, enabling a comprehensive analysis of our social media data. Despite facing challenges in merging dashboards, we worked together to solve these issues, ensuring a seamless visualization experience.

Towards the end of our internship, we focused on advanced data analysis and visualization using pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn. We conducted correlation testing and plotted graphs to uncover insights from the data. These visualizations provided a clear and impactful way to present the results of our analysis.

Overall, this group internship has been an enriching experience for all of us. Working together, we gained essential skills in data analysis, visualization, and project management. This collaborative journey has prepared us to tackle future projects with confidence, a strong analytical approach, and the invaluable experience of teamwork.
